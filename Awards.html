Awards & Recognitions

Honours that reflect trust, service, and compassion in action.

Every recognition is a reminder of the community that stands with Ni Aasre Da Aasra: local service clubs, spiritual organizations, media platforms, and social bodies that have acknowledged the shelter's care for people who need it most.

10 recognitions displayed from the shelter's award collection
Recognition Gallery

Celebrating the hands that serve quietly every day.

The awards below honour humanitarian service, social commitment, public awareness, spiritual values, and practical support for shelter residents. Together, they tell the story of a home built through compassion and community faith.

YouTube Silver Creator Award

Presented for crossing 100,000 subscribers, this recognition reflects how the shelter's rescue stories and humanitarian work have reached a wider public audience.
