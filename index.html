Our Services

Where caring is a way of life.

Our approach blends compassion and action, working with hospitals, police, volunteers, and donors so no person is left to suffer alone.

Shelter and daily care

Safe rooms, clean clothing, hygiene support, and daily routines that help residents feel protected and cared for.

Medical support

Doctor visits, medicine, wound care, recovery monitoring, and hospital coordination for people who need urgent help.

Nutritious meals

Fresh meals, hydration, and diet support served with consistency so every person has strength through the day.

Volunteer programs

Structured opportunities to serve, assist with care activities, support events, and bring companionship to residents.

About Us

A home built from one life-changing act of compassion.

Ni Aasre Da Aasra is a humanitarian NGO and shelter home in Yamuna Nagar, Haryana, dedicated to people who have been left without family, safety, or care. Founded by Jaskirat Singh with the support of Balwinder Kaur, it grew from a personal farmhouse into a sanctuary for homeless, abandoned, mentally distressed, elderly, and physically ill people.

The team rescues people from roadsides, hospitals, and neglected places, then provides shelter, meals, medical support, rehabilitation, and daily care with dignity. The goal is simple and deeply human: to make every person feel seen, protected, and part of a family again.
