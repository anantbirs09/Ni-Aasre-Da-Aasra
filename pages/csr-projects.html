What is Ni Aasre Da Aasra?

A registered shelter serving the most vulnerable.

Ni Aasre Da Aasra Society, Mustafabad, actively rescues, shelters, and rehabilitates homeless, destitute, mentally ill, and abandoned people from Haryana and neighbouring states.

24/7 Rescue Operations

Emergency rescues from roadsides, railway platforms, hospitals, and police stations across 40+ districts.

Shelter & Care

Safe accommodation with food, clothing, sanitation, and daily care for 200+ residents.

Free Medical Treatment

On-site and referral medical care for residents arriving malnourished, injured, or critical.

Women Empowerment

Dedicated safe space for abandoned and vulnerable women with protection and rehabilitation.

Family Reunification

Identifying and reconnecting recovered residents with families across India.

Skill Restoration

Vocational training in tailoring, handicrafts, and trades for independent reintegration.

FAQ's: CSR Queries

Answers for CSR, finance, and legal teams.

Can our company legally route CSR funds to Ni Aasre Da Aasra?

Yes. The organisation has valid registrations and can provide the documents required for annual CSR reporting.

What documents will we receive after contribution?

Donation receipt, tax exemption certificate, Schedule VII compliance letter, fund utilisation report, and project impact report.

Can we visit the shelter before committing funds?

Yes. CSR teams, CFOs, and leadership can schedule a guided facility visit at Village Magharpur, Mustafabad, Yamunanagar.

How often will we receive impact reports?

Quarterly impact reports with photographs, beneficiary counts, and fund utilisation breakdowns are provided as standard.
