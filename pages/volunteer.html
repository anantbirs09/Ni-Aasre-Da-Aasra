Volunteer / Work With Us

Serve with care, discipline, and responsibility.

Ni Aasre Da Aasra works with vulnerable residents, so volunteer entries must be serious, verified, and suitable for shelter work.

Who Can Apply?

We welcome people who can commit time responsibly and respect the dignity, privacy, and safety of every resident.

  • Minimum age: 18 years
  • Valid government ID required
  • Emergency contact required
  • Prior experience is helpful but not compulsory
  • Medical, counselling, teaching, cooking, admin, driving, and field-work skills are especially useful

Final approval may include a phone call, ID verification, and an orientation visit before any shelter duty is assigned.
